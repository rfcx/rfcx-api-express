Privacy Policy

Rainforest Connection respects your privacy. It is Rainforest Connection's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website, https://rfcx.org and other sites we own and operate.

What information do we collect from you? The types of information we collect includes: your full name, email address, address, donation amount, favorite guardian streams and likes.

When do we collect information? We keep your contact information when you sign up for our mailing list via the Rainforest Connection player app or website. We record your address and donation amount when you make a donation.

How do we use this information? We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. We also let you know why we’re collecting it and how it will be used.

If you have signed up for our mailing list we will use your contact information to keep you informed of news related to Rainforest Connection.

What are the lawful bases for collecting your information? We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide you with your requested service. What data we store, we’ll protect within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorised access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.

Security precautions in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of your information. We take appropriate measures to ensure that the information disclosed to us is kept secure, accurate and up-to-date and kept only for so long as is necessary for the purposes for which it is used, as stated above. As an additional security measure, your personal information is also encrypted during transmission by using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) 128-bit encryption software.

When you give us personal information, we take steps to ensure that it is treated securely:

  • Electronic records are kept on a password controlled and restricted access area of Google Cloud or Amazon Web Services. Mailing list names and email addresses are stored in MailChimp. Google Drive and MailChimp are GDPR compliant.
  • Electronic versions and records are kept on secure computers and accessed through password protected devices.

Who has access to your information? We don’t share any of your information publicly or with third-parties, except when required to by law. Personal data is accessible to the Rainforest Connection founders and administrators at Rainforest Connection for the purposes described above.

Our website may link to external sites that are not operated by us. Please be aware that we have no control over the content and practices of these sites, and cannot accept responsibility or liability for their respective privacy policies.

You are free to refuse our request for your personal information, with the understanding that we may be unable to provide you with some of your desired services. Your continued use of our website will be regarded as acceptance of our practices around privacy and personal information. If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us at privacy@rfcx.org .

How you can access and update your information? You can update your information held on the mailing list or unsubscribe by following the link at the bottom of every mailing list email. You have the right to ask for a copy of the information that Rainforest Connection holds about you (we may charge for information requests to cover our costs in providing you with details of the information we hold about you). Requests should be made by email to privacy@rfcx.org .

If you are unhappy with the way you have been treated in relation to the data we collect and our processing of it please contact us so we can attempt to resolve it. If necessary, information on how you can raise a complaint is outlined here .

Review of this Policy We keep this Policy under regular review. This Policy was last updated on August 8, 2019.
