RFCx Arbimon

With the recent launch of RFCx Arbimon you can now upload and analyze an unlimited amount of audio from your AudioMoth and other devices.

Get started now

Includes:

  • Visualizer - an intuitive viewer to review spectrograms, listen to recordings, and annotate audio.
  • Soundscapes - aggregate recordings at different temporal and spatial scales.
  • Pattern matching species identification - automatically search your audio library for a matching spectrogram.
  • Data organization - create playlists by site, time of day, detected species, soundscapes, and tags.

RFCx Companion

Deploying AudioMoths in the field? Use the Companion to take advantage of the following features:

  • Manage, track and deploy AudioMoths from your Android phone
  • Recordings will be automatically linked to deployment geo-location (using Uploader)
  • Attach photos to your device deployment location to mark where you left it

RFCx Uploader

The RFCx Uploader is compatible with any bioacoustic recording device. Upload any of your audio recordings into Arbimon and benefit from the following features of our Uploader:

  • Auto-Detect - drag & drop your files without having to specify the file name format. Uploader supports all filename formats for commonly used bioacoustic devices (SongMeter, AudioMoth, Swift etc).
  • Upload in the background - run your uploads as a background process, while you continue to do analysis in Arbimon.
  • Pause and resume - uploads can be paused when in an area of poor connectivity and resumed at a later time without anything being lost.
